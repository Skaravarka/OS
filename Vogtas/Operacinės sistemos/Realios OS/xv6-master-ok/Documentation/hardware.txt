## Keyboard

kbd.c uses [Keyboard Scan Codes: Set 1][1]. Sectoins `Scan Codes` and `Make Codes,
Break Codes, and Typematic Repeat` in [The PS/2 Keyboard Interface][2] help the 
understanding of the code.

[1]: http://www.computer-engineering.org/ps2keyboard/scancodes1.html
[2]: http://www.computer-engineering.org/ps2keyboard/
